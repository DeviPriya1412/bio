VIJAY(ACTOR)

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar (born 22 June 1974[2][3]), known professionally as Vijay, is an Indian actor and singer who works predominantly in Tamil cinema.[4][5] He is among the highest paid actors in India[6][7] and has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list on seven occasions.[8][9] He has played the lead in 66 films and the International Business Times framed him as a "consistent performer".[10] Referred to as Thalapathy (transl. Commander), Vijay has a significant following internationally.[11][6] He has won several awards, including an Osaka Best Actor Award and a South Indian International Movie Award.[12][13] In 2023, he became the highest paid actor in India and one of the top paid actors in the world

EARLY LIFE AND FAMILY

Vijay was born as Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar on 22 June 1974 in Madras (now Chennai), Tamil Nadu.[15] His father, S. A. Chandrasekhar, is a Tamil film director[16] and his mother, Shoba Chandrasekhar, is a playback singer and carnatic vocalist.[17] His father is of Christian Vellalar descent and his mother is a Hindu.[18][19] Vijay was baptised at the age of 12.[18] Vijay had a sister, Vidhya, who died when she was just two years old.[20] Initially attending Fathima Matriculation Higher Secondary School[21] at Kodambakkam, Vijay later joined the Balalok Matriculation Higher Secondary School[22] at Virugambakkam and went on to pursue a degree in Visual Communications from Loyola College. While studying, at the age of 18, he asked his father if "he would launch him"

PERSONAL LIFE

Vijay married Sangeetha Sornalingam,[151][152] a Sri Lankan Tamil whom he met in the United Kingdom, on 25 August 1999.[153][154] They have two children.[155] Vijay's son, made a cameo appearance with his father in Vettaikaaran (2009) and his daughter portrayed a small role as her father's pre-teen daughter in the climax of Theri (2016).
